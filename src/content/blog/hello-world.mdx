Welcome to my blog! I'm excited to start sharing my thoughts, learnings, and experiences as a developer.

## Why I'm Starting a Blog

I've been developing for a while now, and I've realized that writing about what I learn helps me:

1. **Solidify my understanding** - Teaching is the best way to learn
2. **Track my progress** - It's nice to look back and see how far I've come
3. **Connect with others** - The dev community is amazing and I want to be part of it

## What to Expect

I'll be writing about:

- **Web Development** - React, Next.js, TypeScript, and modern frontend techniques
- **Design** - UI/UX thoughts, accessibility, and making things look good
- **Career** - Lessons learned, tips for other developers
- **Projects** - Walkthroughs of things I'm building
